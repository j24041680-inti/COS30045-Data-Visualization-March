How to Read the Energy Rating Label

The Australian Energy Rating Label is a mandatory guide found on all new televisions and whitegoods.

1

The Stars

More stars = More efficient. Gold stars indicate "Super Efficient" models (5-6 stars).

2

Energy Consumption

The number in the box shows kWh per year. This is what you actually pay for.
