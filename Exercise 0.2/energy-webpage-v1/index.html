Understanding the Star Rating

In Australia, the Energy Rating Label helps you compare the energy efficiency and running costs of appliances before you buy. The more stars, the more efficient the product is.

What to look for:

  • The Stars: More stars = Lower running costs.
  • Energy Consumption: This number (kWh per year) tells you how much electricity it uses.
  • Comparison: Only compare models of a similar size or capacity.

About Energy Wise Australia

Welcome to Energy Wise Australia, your independent resource for navigating the landscape of home appliance efficiency.

Our Mission

Our goal is simple: to empower Australian households with the data they need to reduce their carbon footprint and lower utility bills. Choosing the right appliance is about the long-term "total cost of ownership."

Why Energy Ratings Matter:

  • Financial Savings: High-efficiency appliances save hundreds annually.
  • Environmental Impact: Lower demand reduces grid-wide emissions.
  • Market Transparency: We provide accurate consumption figures (kWh/year).

The Australian Context

Australia has some of the most rigorous energy rating standards in the world. The Star Rating system helps consumers compare products like televisions and refrigerators across different climate zones.
